AISF

The Italian Association of Physics Students promotes collaborations between Italian physica students in an international and global context. Its members are organized into Local Committees and individual members, they meet up periodically in conferences and local, national and international events.

IAPS

L'International Association of Physics Students is an umbrella organization which, since 1987 promotes collaboration between students and physics associations all over the world. Its members are represented by National and Local Committees. IAPS represents approximately 50000 students and AISF is Italy's National COmmittee.
