Associazione Italiana Studenti di Fisica

The Italian Association of Physics Students (AISF) is a non-profit organization with educational purposes which aims to bring together all Italian physics students. It countss more than 900 members and 12 local committees; It is a National Committee of the International Association of Physics Students (IAPS).
